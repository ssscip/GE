About the Project

Global Monitoring 2000-2025 is an innovative data visualization project developed for the NASA Space Apps Challenge, showcasing a quarter-century of Earth observation data. By harnessing the power of NASA's extensive satellite network, we've created a comprehensive timeline of our planet's environmental evolution.

Our project combines data from multiple NASA missions, including Terra, Aqua, and the Suomi NPP satellites, to create an unprecedented view of Earth's changing face. Through interactive visualizations and detailed analysis, we highlight significant environmental events, climate patterns, and long-term trends that have shaped our world.

This platform serves as both a scientific resource and a public education tool, making complex environmental data accessible to everyone. By presenting 25 years of observations in an engaging format, we aim to foster understanding of our planet's dynamic systems and inspire action for environmental preservation.
